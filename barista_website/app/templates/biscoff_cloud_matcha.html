Biscoff Cloud Matcha Latte

Welcome to the recipe page for making biscoff matcha latte!

Get ready to embrace the interesting combination of the caramelized flavor of Biscoff cookies and cookie butter with the earthy, slightly bitter taste of matcha, with an added crunch of the cookie topping! Biscoff is refer to a brand of speculoos cookies, known for their deep caramel flavour and a hint of cinnamon. The Biscoff cookie butter, a spreadable version of the cookie, adds a rich sweet, and buttery element to the drink.

Please visit matcha preparation tips and cream preparation tips before proceeding with this recipe for a more efficient preparation 😄.

Ingredients

Biscoff cream:

  • 2 tbsp of oat milk or other plant-based milk of your choice
  • 1/4 cup / 60 ml of heavy cream
  • 1/2 tbsp of Biscoff spread
  • Biscoff cookie

Matcha:

  • 4 g of matcha powder
  • 1/6 cup / 40 ml of hot water (~70-80Â°C)
  • 1/3 cup / 80 ml of oat milk or other plant-based milk of your choice
  • 1 cup / 1 large block of ice
  • 1/2 tbsp of Biscoff spread

Equipment

  • Measuring spoons (optional)
  • Microwave / Kettle with adjustable temperature (optional)
  • Microwave lid / any container lid
  • Pestle and mortar / Blender (optional)
  • Thermometer (optional)
  • Sieve
  • Bowl / Chawan
  • bamboo spoon / chashaku
  • Bamboo whisk
  • Hand-held frother
  • Frothing pitcher / Cup or bowl for cream preparation (optional)

Recipe

Preparation Time:

15 mins

Cook time:

5 mins

Total time:

20 mins

Servings:

1 person

Matcha:

  1. Sift the matcha powder into a bowl/chawan using a sieve and a bamboo spoon/chashaku to prevent clumps.
  2. Microwave the water while covering it with a microwave lid cover/any container lid. Test with a thermometer every 10 seconds to adjust the heating time. (Mine took around 20 seconds to get to the rigth temperature). If it gets too hot, you could leave it to cool down for a few minutes. Alternatively, if you have a kettle with an adjustable temperature, you could just heat water with it.
  3. Add the water into the bowl/chawan containing the matcha powder and whisk it using a bamboo whisk.

Base:

  1. Microwave the Biscoff spread to melt it.
  2. Spread 1/2 of the Biscoff spread from the base of the cup to the top edges for decoration.
  3. Add ice to the cup for the drink.
  4. Pour the milk into the cup.
  5. Pour the whisked matcha on the surface of the milk.

Cream:

  1. In a frothing pitcher or a separate cup/bowl, add in heavy cream and the milk.
  2. Add in the other half of the melted Biscoff spread.
  3. Whisk using a hand-held frother until the mixture is well-blended and creamy.

Final preparation:

  1. Pour the cream mixture into the cup for the drink. Tips on pouring: Gently pour the cream onto the tips of the ice to avoid mixing the cream with the latte. This will create the floating effect. Pour in a circular motion.
  2. Crush / Blend the cookie using the pestle and mortar / blender for the top decoration.
  3. Scoop the crushed cookie bits onto the cream.
  4. Enjoy!
You can substitute matcha with hojicha to make biscoff cloud hojicha latte!
