Biscoff Cloud Matcha Latte

Welcome to the recipe page for making pistachio cloud matcha latte!

Taste the nutty, sweet flavour of pistachio with the classic bitterness of the matcha in this creation --- Pistachio cloud matcha latte!

Pistachio has been a recent viral sensation in culinary desserts and beverages. It is especially the star of the famous Dubai chocolate that is favoured by dessert lovers like me!

Pistachio is derived from the Spanish and Portugese word --- "Pistacho". It is a type of tree nutt which is widely consumed as a food, either raw, roasted, or salted. Pistachios are known for being a good source of protein, healthy fats, fiber, and variouus vitamins and minerals, including vitamin B6, vitamin E, potassium, magnesium and zinc. Pistachios are linked to several potential health benefits, including supporting healthy cholesterol levels, potentially lowering blood pressure, and contributing to healthy sugar levels. They also contain antioxidants.

Ingredients

Pistachio cream:

  • 2 tbsp of oat milk or other plant-based milk of your choice
  • 1/4 cup / 60 ml of heavy cream
  • 1/2 tbsp of Pistacho spread
  • Pistachios (optional)

Matcha:

  • 4 g of matcha powder
  • 1/6 cup / 40 ml of hot water (~70-80Â°C)
  • 1/3 cup / 80 ml of oat milk or other plant-based milk of your choice
  • 1 cup / 1 large block of ice
  • 1/2 tbsp of Pistachio spread

Equipment

  • Measuring spoons (optional)
  • Microwave / Kettle with adjustable temperature (optional)
  • Microwave lid / any container lid
  • Pestle and mortar / Blender (optional)
  • Thermometer (optional)
  • Sieve
  • Bowl / Chawan
  • bamboo spoon / chashaku
  • Bamboo whisk
  • Hand-held frother
  • Frothing pitcher / Cup or bowl for cream preparation (optional)

Recipe

Preparation Time:

15 mins

Cook time:

5 mins

Total time:

20 mins

Servings:

1 person

Matcha:

  1. Sift the matcha powder into a bowl/chawan using a sieve and a bamboo spoon/chashaku to prevent clumps.
  2. Microwave the water while covering it with a microwave lid cover/any container lid. Test with a thermometer every 10 seconds to adjust the heating time. (Mine took around 20 seconds to get to the rigth temperature). If it gets too hot, you could leave it to cool down for a few minutes. Alternatively, if you have a kettle with an adjustable temperature, you could just heat water with it.
  3. Add the water into the bowl/chawan containing the matcha powder and whisk it using a bamboo whisk.

Base:

  1. Microwave the Pistachio spread to melt it.
  2. Spread 1/2 of the Pistachio spread from the base of the cup to the top edges for decoration.
  3. Add ice to the cup for the drink.
  4. Pour the milk into the cup.
  5. Pour the whisked matcha on the surface of the milk.

Cream:

  1. In a frothing pitcher or a separate cup/bowl, add in heavy cream and the milk.
  2. Add in the other half of the melted Pistachio spread.
  3. Whisk using a hand-held frother until the mixture is well-blended and creamy.

Final preparation:

  1. Pour the cream mixture into the cup for the drink. Tips on pouring: Gently pour the cream onto the tips of the ice to avoid mixing the cream with the latte. This will create the floating effect. Pour in a circular motion.
  2. Optional: Crush / Blend the pistachios using the pestle and mortar / blender for the top decoration.
  3. Scoop the crushed pistachios onto the cream.
  4. Enjoy!
You can substitute matcha with hojicha to make pistachio cloud hojicha latte!
