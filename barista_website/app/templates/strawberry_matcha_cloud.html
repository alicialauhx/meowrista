Strawberry Matcha Cloud Latte

Welcome to my recipe page for making strawberry matcha cloud latte! Strawberry matcha has been a viral sensation for matcha lovers and there have been lots of versions of making it. But why not add an interesting twist --- floating matcha cloud!!

A strawberry matcha cloud latte is a vibrant and refreshing drink that combines the earthy, slightly bitter notes of matcha with the sweet and fruity flavor of strawberries. Typically, it involves layering a strawberry puree with milk and matcha, ofthen served over ice for a cool and visually appealing beverage. The sweetness of the strawberries helps to balance the matcha's natural bitterness, creating a harmonious and delicious flavor profile.

Ingredients:

Matcha cream:

  • 4 g of matcha powder
  • 2 tbsp of oat milk or other plant-based milk of your choice
  • 1/4 cup / 60 ml of heavy cream

Strawberry latte:

  • 4 ripe strawberries
  • 1/2 tbsp honey / other sweeteners of your choice
  • 1/3 / 80 ml of oat milk or other plant-based milk of you choice
  • 1 cup / 1 large block of ice

Equipment

  • Measuring spoons (optional)
  • Pestle and mortar / Blender (optional)
  • Sieve
  • Bowl / Chawan
  • bamboo spoon / Chashaku
  • Hand-held frother
  • Frothing pitcher / Cup or bowl for cream preparation (optional)

Recipe

Preparation Time:

5 mins

Cook time:

5 mins

Total time:

10 mins

Servings:

1 person

Base:

  1. Crush/Blend 3 strawberries using a pestle and mortar/blender to make the puree for the base of the drink.
  2. Add ice into the cup for the drink.
  3. Drizzle honey/other sweeteners of your choice into the cup.
  4. Add milk to the cup.

Cream:

  1. In a frothing pitcher or a separate cup/bowl, add in heavy cream and the milk.
  2. Sift in the matcha powder using a sieve and a bamboo spoon/Chashaku to prevent clumps.
  3. Whisk using a hand-held frother until the mixture is well-blended and creamy.

Final preparation:

  1. Pour the cream mixture into the cup for the drink. Tips on pouring: Gently pour the cream onto the tips of the ice to avoid mixing the cream with the latte. This is to create the floating effect. Pour in a circular motion.
  2. Slice the bottom tip of the strawberry halfway with a knife to prepare the decoration for the drink. Place the strawberry on the edge of the drink for a fancy finish.
  3. Enjoy!
You can substitute matcha with hojicha to make hojicha ube cloud latte!
