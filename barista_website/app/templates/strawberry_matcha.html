Strawberry Matcha Latte

Welcome to my recipe page for making strawberry matcha latte! Strawberry matcha has been a viral sensation for matcha lovers and there have been lots of versions of making it.

A strawberry matcha latte is a vibrant and refreshing drink that combines the earthy, slightly bitter notes of matcha with the sweet and fruity flavor of strawberries. Typically, it involves layering a strawberry puree with milk and matcha, ofthen served over ice for a cool and visually appealing beverage. The sweetness of the strawberries helps to balance the matcha's natural bitterness, creating a harmonious and delicious flavor profile.

Ingredients:

Latte:

  • 4 g of matcha powder
  • 4 ripe strawberries
  • 1/2 tbsp honey / other sweeteners of your choice
  • 1/3 / 80 ml of oat milk or other plant-based milk of you choice
  • 1 cup / 1 large block of ice

Equipment

  • Measuring spoons (optional)
  • Pestle and mortar / Blender (optional)
  • Sieve
  • Bowl / Chawan
  • Bamboo spoon / Chashaku
  • Frothing pitcher / Cup or bowl for cream preparation (optional)

Recipe

Preparation Time:

5 mins

Cook time:

5 mins

Total time:

10 mins

Servings:

1 person

Base:

  1. Crush/Blend 3 strawberries using a pestle and mortar/blender to make the puree for the base of the drink.
  2. Add ice into the cup for the drink.
  3. Drizzle honey/other sweeteners of your choice into the cup.
  4. Add milk to the cup.

Matcha:

  1. Sift the matcha powder into a bowl/chawan using a sieve and a bamboo spoon/chashaku to prevent clumps.
  2. Microwave the water while covering it with a microwave lid cover/any container lid. Test with a thermometer every 10 seconds to adjust the heating time. (Mine took around 20 seconds to get to the rigth temperature). If it gets too hot, you could leave it to cool down for a few minutes. Alternatively, if you have a kettle with an adjustable temperature, you could just heat water with it.
  3. Add the water into the bowl/chawan containing the matcha powder and whisk it using a bamboo whisk.

Final preparation:

  1. Pour the whisked matcha on the surface of the milk.
  2. Slice the bottom tip of the strawberry halfway with a knife to prepare the decoration for the drink. Place the strawberry on the edge of the drink for a fancy finish.
  3. Enjoy!
You can substitute matcha with hojicha to make hojicha ube cloud latte!
