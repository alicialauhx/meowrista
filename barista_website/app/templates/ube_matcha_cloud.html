Ube Matcha Cloud Latte

Welcome to the recipe page for making ube matcha cloud latte!

An ube matcha cloud latte is a vibrant and flavorful beverage that combines the earthy notes of matcha with the sweet, nutty flavor of ube, a purple yam from the Philippines. This visually appealing drink is typically layer, with the bright purple ube milk forming a creamy base and the green matcha creating a contrasting top layer. It is a popular choice for those seeking a unique and visually striking drink with a delightful balance of flavors.

Ingredients

Matcha cream:

  • 4 g of matcha powder
  • 2 tbsp of oat milk or other plant-based milk of your choice
  • 1/4 cup / 60 ml of heavy cream

Ube latte:

  • 1/3 cup / 80 ml of oat milk or other plant-based milk of your choice
  • 1 cup / 1 large block of ice
  • 1/2 tbsp of ube paste/halaya
  • A few drops of ube syrup/extract

Equipment

  • Measuring spoons (optional)
  • Sieve
  • Bowl / Chawan
  • Wooden spoon / chashaku
  • Hand-held frother
  • Frothing pitcher / Cup or bowl for cream preparation (optional)

Recipe

Preparation Time:

5 mins

Cook time:

5 mins

Total time:

10 mins

Servings:

1 person

Base:

  1. Add ube paste/halaya into the cup for the drink.
  2. Sift in the matcha powder using a sieve and a wooden spoon/chashaku to prevent clumps.
  3. Add ice to the cup.
  4. Add milk to the cup.
  5. Add in ube syrup/extract.

Cream:

  1. In a frothing pitcher or a separate cup/bowl, add in heavy cream and the milk.
  2. Sift in the matcha powder using a sieve and a wooden spoon/chashaku to prevent clumps.
  3. Whisk using a hand-held frother until the mixture is well-blended and creamy.

Final preparation:

  1. Pour the cream mixture into the cup for the drink. Tips on pouring: Gently pour the cream onto the tips of the ice to avoid mixing the cream with the latte. This is to create the floating effect. Pour in a circular motion.
  2. Enjoy!
You can substitute matcha with hojicha to make ube hojicha cloud latte!
